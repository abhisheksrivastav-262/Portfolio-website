Got Questions?

We Have Answers

Find answers to the most common questions about our services, pricing, and processes.

What services does Maintruction Company offer?

We offer a complete range of home maintenance services including Plumbing, Electrical, Carpentry, AC Installation & Repair, Interior & Exterior Painting, Civil Work, Waterproofing, Home Renovation, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).

Which areas in Ahmedabad do you serve?

We serve all areas across Ahmedabad and its surrounding regions including Satellite, SG Highway, Prahlad Nagar, Bodakdev, Thaltej, Navrangpura, Maninagar, Gota, Chandkheda, Bopal, and more.

Do you provide emergency services?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency services for urgent plumbing leaks, electrical failures, and other critical home repair issues. Call us at +91 8460617976 for immediate assistance.

How do I book a service?

You can book a service by calling us directly, sending a WhatsApp message to +91 8460617976, or filling out the contact form on our website. Our team will schedule a visit at your earliest convenience.

What are your working hours?

Our regular working hours are Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Sunday services are available on prior appointment. Emergency services are available 24/7.

Do you provide a cost estimate before starting work?

Absolutely. We always provide a detailed, transparent cost estimate after inspecting the job. There are no hidden charges. Work begins only after you approve the quotation.

Are your technicians trained and verified?

Yes, all our technicians are background-verified, highly trained, and have several years of experience. We ensure every professional meets our strict quality standards before they join our team.

Do you offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)?

Yes, we offer customizable Annual Maintenance Contracts for homes, offices, and residential societies. Our AMC plans cover regular maintenance, priority support, and discounted repair rates.

What types of AC units do you service?

We service and repair all types of air conditioners including Split AC, Window AC, Cassette AC, Tower AC, and Central AC systems from all major brands like Daikin, Voltas, LG, Samsung, Hitachi, and more.

Do you use quality materials for repairs?

Yes, we only use ISI-certified, high-quality, branded materials and spare parts. We believe in long-lasting repairs that save you money in the long run.

Can you handle large-scale renovation projects?

Absolutely. We have dedicated project managers and skilled teams for large-scale home renovations, office interiors, complete bathroom/kitchen remodeling, and civil construction work.

Do you provide a warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide a service warranty on all our work. The warranty period varies by service type — typically 30 days for minor repairs and up to 1 year for major works like waterproofing and painting.

What is the typical response time for a service request?

For regular service requests, we typically schedule a visit within 24–48 hours. For emergencies, our response time is usually within 1–2 hours depending on your location.

Can I customize a modular kitchen or wardrobe design?

Yes, our carpentry team works closely with you to create fully customized modular kitchens, wardrobes, and furniture tailored to your space, style preferences, and budget.

Do you handle waterproofing for terraces and bathrooms?

Yes, we specialize in terrace waterproofing, bathroom waterproofing, wall seepage treatment, and crack filling using advanced chemical coatings with long-term warranties.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS), and Cheque payments. For larger projects, we also offer milestone-based payment plans.

Do you provide services for commercial properties?

Yes, we cater to both residential and commercial properties including offices, shops, showrooms, restaurants, and residential societies with customized maintenance solutions.

What brands of paint do you use?

We use premium paints from trusted brands like Asian Paints, Berger, Nerolac, and Dulux. We can also work with any specific brand you prefer.

How long does a typical home painting project take?

A standard 2-BHK interior painting takes 4–5 days including wall putty, primer, and two coats of paint. Larger homes or texture/decorative work may take 7–10 days.

How do I get a quote for my project?

Simply call us at +91 8460617976, WhatsApp us, or fill out the form on our Contact page. Our expert will visit your site for a free inspection and provide a detailed, no-obligation quotation.
